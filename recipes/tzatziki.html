Tzatziki

Description

Tzatziki is a sauce popular in Greek cuisine. It is made simply with yogurt, cumcumber, olive, oil, and fresh herbs. It can also be used as a dip or spread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated cucumber
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp EVOO
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt

Steps

  1. Remove excess moisture from cucumber and add to a large bowl.
  2. Add yogurt, EVOO, herbs, lemon juice, garlic, and salt to the bowl. Mix well.
  3. Let mixture sit for 5 minutes. Then serve. Store in refrigerator for up to 4 days.
