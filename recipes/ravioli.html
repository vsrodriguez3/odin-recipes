Ravioli

Description

Ravioli consists of a wrapping made of pasta that can be filled with meat, cheese, or vegetables. Ravioli is typically square-shaped but can also have other shapes. It is considered an Italian "dumpling."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Ravioli filling

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk flour, salt, and eggs.
  2. Add olive oil and knead dough for 5 minutes. Then, leave dough in bowl covered with plastic wrap for 30 min.
  3. Take a small piece of dough and use a rolling pin to create a thin sheet. Use a cookie cutter to cut circles.
  4. Place 1 tsp of ravioli filling in the center of half of the circles. Place the second circle on top over the filling. You may use a fork to crimp the edges.
  5. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add ravioli. Once they float, they are done.
